Welcome!

...to Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock. A game in which players choose between 5 different fighting options to best their opponents. Each option comes with its own qualities and abilities that can either best or be bested by the other four options. The different options along with the rules they follow are set below. Every time a round is one, the round-winning player gains a point. The first to win three point is the overall winner of the game, and if the two players choose the same then it is a draw, and no points are awarded. The game is played between two people, a player one and a player two. Good luck to both!
